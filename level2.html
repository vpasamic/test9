Welcome to the next level of Flexbox Hearts! Now that you've learned about flex-direction: column;, let's dive into another important Flexbox property: justify-content.

The justify-content property aligns the flex items along the main axis of the flex container.

Here are some common values for justify-content:

  • flex-start: Aligns items at the start of the container.
  • flex-end: Aligns items at the end of the container.
  • center: Centers items in the container.
  • space-between: Distributes items evenly with space between them.
  • space-around: Distributes items evenly with space around them.
